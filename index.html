NxN QUEEN

An NxN Queen is the most versatile piece in chess, combining the movements of the Rook and Bishop, able to traverse diagonally, horizontally, and vertically across the board. Its ability to control vast swathes of the board makes it a potent force in both offense and defense

NxN Bishop

A NxN Bishop is a chess piece capable of moving diagonally across the board, covering any distance in a single direction until obstructed by another piece or reaching the edge. Its power lies in its ability to control long diagonals, influencing vast areas of the board.

NxN Knight

In chess, an NxN Knight moves in an L-shape: two squares in one direction and one square perpendicular. It's unique for its ability to leap over other pieces, offering tactical maneuvers and the potential to quickly traverse the board.

NxN Rook

An NxN Rook is a powerful chess piece that moves horizontally or vertically across the board, covering any distance until obstructed. Its straightforward movement makes it a valuable asset for controlling ranks and files and executing strategic attacks.
